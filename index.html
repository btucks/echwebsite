Our Mission

To grow the game of hockey at all levels, with an emphasis on promoting collegiate hockey for both men and women. College hockey has been producing great hockey players and even better people since its inception, and we hope to spread that awareness nationwide. Attend one college hockey game and feel the school spirit after the home team scores and their fight song echoes throughout the rink. The connection between fans, students, friends, and family with each team is unmatched, and ECH is there to cover every special moment with one of a kind interviews, gameday atmosphere captures, and more.

Meet the Team

James started Everything College Hockey back in 2016. As a former college hockey player he knows how special college hockey can be as a player and as a fan.

Steven makes sure you get a chance to know all of the most exciting players behind the scenes. He is the host of the Everything College Hockey Podcast where he chats with players about their school, stories, and advice for young players trying to pave their own path in hockey.

Julia knows anything and everything going on throughout the college hockey world. She is involved in interviewing players post game and making sure the best highlights of every game are available every Sunday. Keep up with Julia on her socials @juliaECH so you don't miss a minute of the most exciting news around the league.
